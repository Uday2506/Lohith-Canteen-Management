Our Story

A Canteen That
Feeds the Campus

Lohit Canteen — the central dining hub of IIT Guwahati — has been feeding dreams since the institute's early days. Nestled at the heart of the campus, we serve a wide variety of North-Eastern, North Indian, South Indian, and Continental dishes, prepared fresh every single day.

From the early morning chai rush to the late-night snack run before exams — Lohit Canteen has been there for every moment of campus life.
